about us

why choose us?

best food in the srilanka

Welcome to Resto, where culinary excellence meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Colombo, our restaurant is more than just a dining destination; it's a celebration of flavors, tradition, and community. From our humble beginnings to our present-day dedication to serving delicious meals, every dish we craft tells a story of passion and commitment.

At Resto, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our journey began 2022, when Ms. Nipuni Chandramali envisioned a place where friends and families could gather to savor mouthwatering dishes made from the freshest ingredients. Today, that vision lives on in every plate we serve, as we continue to uphold our commitment to quality and authenticity.

Our culinary team, led by Executive Chef Salgadu, draws inspiration from local ingredients to create a menu that delights the senses and nourishes the soul. Whether you're joining us for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, we invite you to experience the magic of Resto. Thank you for being a part of our story; we look forward to welcoming you into our warm and inviting space.
